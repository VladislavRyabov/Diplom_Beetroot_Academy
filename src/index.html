EXPERIENCE AND PRACTICE

SENCE ARCHITECTS is a studio with 8 years of experience in the design and implementation of architectural and interior projects of various levels of complexity all over the world. Our team of talented artists, architects, designers, planners, and engineers works as a perfect mechanism that has been perfecting it`s coordination over the years. We love experiments. Any challenge is a new opportunity to create an ideal living space and make our client`s dreams to come true.

SCIENCE OF ARCHITECTURE

Each project is approached as a new scientific challenge. Before designing we devote a separate time for a study of our client`s lifestyle. The deeper we understand all the pain points and preferences of the client’s everyday life, the better we can design his or her space. Our goal is to create a living environment, where every square meter is used functionally, and money is spent effectively.

PERSONALIZATION AND PRIVACY

We guarantee complete confidentiality and privacy of communication to our clients. Every customer becomes a part of our SENCE family.
